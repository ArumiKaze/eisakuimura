Shroommates

Overview

Save the mushroom family from the humans in Shroommates, a light-hearted 3D platforming adventure where players solve puzzles through clever use of the environment and tries to get everyone back home safely. The player is a little shroom in a big world—squish down for a powerful jump, parachute away with the mushroom cap, and climb vines to get to vantage points around the house. Beware of the human chef bent on putting delicious shrooms on his pizza, and a deadly Shroomba that attacks fungi on sight! Shroommates is full of funny interactions to discover, catchy music, and a fully voiced cast of colorful characters to meet. This project was developed in Unreal Engine 4 with a team of 8 people over the course of roughly 1 year.

Platform: PC
Engine/Languages: Unreal Engine 4 (C++/Blueprints)
Development Time: 1 year 1 month
Year of Completion: 2018
Team Size: 8

Notes

Programming responsibilities ranged over different tasks such as implementing the upgrade system, in-game store, interactable items, environmental hazards, player locomotion, and various other player mechanics using a combination of C++ and blueprints. Constantly communicating with the designers was a crucial component of development to make sure the mechanics I implemented matched their designs and I exposed specific functions/variables in blueprints to make sure the designers could fine tune the mechanic without having to go into code.

Please feel free to download the game and try it out! I have also provided the Github repository if you would like to observe that as well.
